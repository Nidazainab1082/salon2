Artika Salon


The Artika website has a fresh and minimalistic design that brings to life their
brand in a beautiful way while simultaneously making the experience for
visitors easy and intuitive. Here are a few things we can learn from Artika:

.Showing salon interior pictures right on the home page. This makes it
obvious to the person landing on the website what type of business it is.
It helps people enter into the Artika experience before even visiting the

.Intuitive navigation and the “book now” button clearly visible (without
looking salesy)

.Fully integrated and intuitive booking flow via their Mangomint
website booking widget that simply fades in over the website.
